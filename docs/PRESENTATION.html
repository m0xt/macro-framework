The framework, in 60 seconds

Posture rule: below −0.50 → CASH / 0%; −0.50 to +0.25 → CAUTION / 75%; above +0.25 → LONG / 100%.

MRMI is built from two pillars — fast market momentum and slow macro stress:

Pillar 1 · MMI (Market Momentum)

What markets are pricing right now. Built from Growth Impulses, Sector Breadth, and Financial Conditions. Moves fast — days to weeks.

Pillar 2 · Macro Stress

What the real economy and inflation trajectory are saying. Calm by default; rises when growth weakens or inflation accelerates, fastest when both happen.

Today: CALM 0.8 / 10

Current read: momentum is constructive and stress is calm, but MRMI is still inside the investor caution zone because the threshold layer demands a stronger cushion before returning to 100% exposure.

How MRMI is computed

g = max(0, −Real_Economy)

i = max(0, Inflation_Direction)

Stress_raw = 0.75g + 0.50i + 10gi

Stress_intensity = clip(Stress_raw / 10.0083, 0, 1)

Macro_buffer = 0.5 × (1 − Stress_intensity)

MRMI = MMI + Macro_buffer − 0.75

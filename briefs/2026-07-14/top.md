The central test the prior brief left unresolved — core CPI and major bank earnings landing on the same morning — resolved on the less damaging end: [the six-month core inflation trend held flat at +0.30](https://www.bls.gov/cpi/), avoiding the worst case where growth weakness and re-accelerating prices arrived simultaneously. That partial relief nudged the stress score to +3.13 from +3.39 and the macro buffer to +0.34 from +0.33, but neither shift was large enough to prevent the headline allocation posture from sliding further to +0.18 from +0.25 — moving deeper into CAUTION at 75% exposure — because market momentum declined more than the economy improved. Breadth fell for a third straight week to +0.98 (seven-day change -0.56) and financial conditions dipped to +0.59 as the VIX climbed to 17.16, pulling market momentum down to +0.59 from +0.67. The one signal cutting across both pillars constructively was growth impulses turning positive for the first time all quarter, rising to +0.19 from -0.33, as the [Baltic Dry Index surged roughly 8%](https://www.handybulk.com/baltic-dry-index/) and the yield curve's quiet steepening to 35 basis points since the June payrolls miss began registering in the growth signal — a development the economy is confirming in its own thin way, with the real economy score improving to -0.79 from -0.87, [GDPNow steadying at 1.3% annualized](https://www.atlantafed.org/research-and-data/data/gdpnow), and initial claims printing a clean 215,000. The net picture is two pillars trending the same direction but at different speeds: the economy stabilizing slowly, the market losing breadth support faster than the macro buffer can rebuild. Getting back above the LONG threshold at +0.25 runs through the advance Q2 GDP print on July 30: a reading above 1.5% that validates the growth impulse, combined with [continuing claims](https://verifiedinvesting.com/blogs/us-economic-metrics/jobless-claims-week-july-4-2026) — already at 1,814,000, their highest since late March — stabilizing or falling, would let both pillars firm together and give the macro buffer room to approach its +0.50 ceiling; if instead claims drift higher while breadth extends its slide, the framework's cushion narrows further and the posture moves toward the lower half of CAUTION.
